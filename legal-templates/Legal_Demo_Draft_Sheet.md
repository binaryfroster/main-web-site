# Legal Demo Draft Sheet (Client Cheat Sheet)

**Purpose:** This document is designed to serve as a quick-reference guide during sales calls or client onboarding. When US clients ask about your legal processes, data security, or intellectual property (IP) handling, you can use these bullet points to answer confidently and professionally.

---

## 1. The Core Legal Process (How We Work)
*When a client asks: "What is your contracting process?"*

*   **Step 1: Mutual NDA (Non-Disclosure Agreement)**
    *   **What you say:** *"Before we dive into the specifics of your proprietary logic or data, we sign a standard mutual NDA to ensure both our trade secrets and your intellectual property are fully protected."*
*   **Step 2: MSA (Master Services Agreement)**
    *   **What you say:** *"We use an MSA to establish the baseline relationship. This covers the heavy lifting—liability, general warranties, and IP assignment. We sign this once."*
*   **Step 3: SOW (Statement of Work)**
    *   **What you say:** *"For every specific project or sprint, we issue a brief SOW. It ties back to the MSA but specifically outlines the deliverables, timeline, and cost. It keeps things agile and clear without needing to renegotiate terms."*

---

## 2. Intellectual Property (IP) Ownership
*When a client asks: "Who owns the code you write for us?"*

*   **The Custom Code:** *"You do. Upon full payment for the deliverable, we assign all rights, title, and interest in the custom software we build specifically for you over to your company."*
*   **Our Pre-existing Tools:** *"To build things faster and more cost-effectively, we utilize our pre-existing libraries and frameworks. You get a perpetual, royalty-free license to use those components as they are integrated into your final product."*
*   **Open Source:** *"We strictly use permissive open-source libraries (like MIT or Apache 2.0) that are safe for commercial, enterprise use. We do not use 'copyleft' (GPL) licenses that would force you to open-source your proprietary product."*

---

## 3. Data Privacy & Security (CCPA / General US Compliance)
*When a client asks: "How do you handle our data and our users' data?"*

*   **Our Policy:** *"We have a strict privacy policy. We only collect the data necessary to perform our services and communicate with you."*
*   **Data Selling:** *"We never sell your data or your users' data to third parties."*
*   **Security Practices:** *"We follow standard industry security practices. For your applications, we implement secure architectures (e.g., proper encryption at rest and in transit) from day one."*

---

## 4. Liability & Risk
*When a client asks: "What happens if something goes wrong?"*

*   **Our Warranty:** *"We warrant that our services are performed in a professional, workmanlike manner according to industry standards."*
*   **Liability Cap:** *"Our standard MSA limits liability to the amount paid for the specific SOW in the preceding months. This is a standard industry practice that protects both parties and keeps costs reasonable."*
