# Master Services Agreement (MSA)

This Master Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of [Date] (the "Effective Date"), by and between **Binary Froster**, with a principal place of business at [Your Address] ("Developer"), and **[Client Name]**, with a principal place of business at [Client Address] ("Client").

## 1. Services
Developer agrees to provide software development, design, and related services ("Services") as specified in individual Statements of Work ("SOW") executed by both parties. Each SOW will define the scope, deliverables, timeline, and fees for a specific project and is incorporated into this Agreement by reference.

## 2. Term
This Agreement will commence on the Effective Date and will remain in full force and effect until terminated by either party in accordance with Section 10.

## 3. Compensation and Payment
*   **Fees:** Client will pay Developer the fees set forth in the applicable SOW.
*   **Invoicing:** Developer will invoice Client according to the schedule in the SOW.
*   **Payment Terms:** Payment is due within [e.g., 15/30] days of the invoice date. Late payments will incur a fee of [e.g., 1.5%] per month.
*   **Expenses:** Client will reimburse Developer for reasonable out-of-pocket expenses incurred in connection with the Services, provided such expenses are pre-approved in writing.

## 4. Independent Contractor
Developer is an independent contractor, not an employee, partner, or agent of Client. Developer is solely responsible for all taxes, withholdings, and other statutory or contractual obligations.

## 5. Intellectual Property Rights
*   **Deliverables:** Upon receipt of full payment for an SOW, Developer assigns to Client all right, title, and interest in and to the specific custom software and materials created for Client under that SOW ("Deliverables").
*   **Pre-existing IP:** Developer retains all rights to its pre-existing tools, libraries, methodologies, and frameworks ("Developer IP"). Developer grants Client a non-exclusive, perpetual, worldwide, royalty-free license to use Developer IP solely as incorporated into the Deliverables.
*   **Open Source:** Deliverables may include open-source software, which will be governed by their respective licenses.

## 6. Confidentiality
Both parties agree to hold each other's Confidential Information in strict confidence. "Confidential Information" includes all non-public technical, business, and financial information. This obligation survives the termination of this Agreement for a period of [e.g., 3] years.

## 7. Warranties
Developer warrants that the Services will be performed in a professional and workmanlike manner. EXCEPT FOR THIS EXPRESS WARRANTY, DEVELOPER DISCLAIMS ALL WARRANTIES, EXPRESS OR IMPLIED, INCLUDING MERCHANTABILITY AND FITNESS FOR A PARTICULAR PURPOSE.

## 8. Limitation of Liability
IN NO EVENT WILL EITHER PARTY BE LIABLE TO THE OTHER FOR ANY INDIRECT, SPECIAL, INCIDENTAL, CONSEQUENTIAL, OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES. DEVELOPER’S TOTAL AGGREGATE LIABILITY ARISING OUT OF OR RELATED TO THIS AGREEMENT WILL NOT EXCEED THE TOTAL AMOUNT PAID BY CLIENT TO DEVELOPER UNDER THE SOW GIVING RISE TO THE CLAIM IN THE [e.g., 6] MONTHS PRECEDING THE CLAIM.

## 9. Indemnification
*   **By Developer:** Developer will indemnify and defend Client against third-party claims alleging that the Deliverables infringe a valid US patent or copyright, provided Client promptly notifies Developer of the claim.
*   **By Client:** Client will indemnify and defend Developer against claims arising from materials provided by Client or Client's use of the Deliverables in violation of this Agreement.

## 10. Termination
*   **For Convenience:** Either party may terminate this Agreement upon [e.g., 30] days written notice if there are no active SOWs.
*   **For Cause:** Either party may terminate this Agreement or an SOW if the other party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure the breach within [e.g., 15] days of written notice.
*   **Effect of Termination:** Upon termination, Client will pay Developer for all Services performed and expenses incurred up to the termination date. Sections 5, 6, 8, 9, and 11 will survive termination.

## 11. General Provisions
*   **Governing Law:** This Agreement will be governed by the laws of the State of [State], without regard to conflict of law principles.
*   **Dispute Resolution:** Any dispute will be resolved by binding arbitration in [City, State] under the rules of the American Arbitration Association.
*   **Entire Agreement:** This Agreement and any executed SOWs constitute the entire agreement between the parties and supersede all prior negotiations or agreements.
*   **Amendments:** Amendments to this Agreement must be in writing and signed by both parties.
